What's included in our management

Everything you need to run profitable Google Ads campaigns, handled by people who do this every day.

Bid & Budget Management

We monitor your campaigns closely and adjust bids based on what the data is telling us. No autopilot, no waiting until the end of the month to make changes.

  • ✓
    Performance-based bid adjustments: Regular changes based on CPA, ROAS, conversion rate, and impression share targets.
  • ✓
    Day-parting & device modifiers: We adjust bids by time of day, day of week, and device based on where your best performance comes from.
  • ✓
    Budget pacing & allocation: We make sure your budget goes where it'll do the most good, and pull back on what isn't performing.

Keyword & Search Term Management

Managing search terms properly is one of the most time-consuming parts of Google Ads, and one of the most important. We stay on top of it so your budget isn't wasted on irrelevant clicks.

  • ✓
    Search term reviews: Regular analysis of what people are actually searching to find new opportunities and add negatives.
  • ✓
    Quality Score monitoring: Tracking Quality Scores over time and working to improve ad relevance, landing page experience, and expected CTR.
  • ✓
    Competitive intelligence: Keeping an eye on auction insights so we know who you're competing against and how to respond.

Reporting & Communication

You'll always know how your campaigns are doing. We send clear, plain-English reports and we're always available if you have questions.

  • ✓
    Regular performance reports: Scheduled reports covering spend, conversions, CPA, ROAS, and impression share with commentary on what it all means.
  • ✓
    Actionable recommendations: Every report includes what we plan to do next and why, so you're never left guessing.
  • ✓
    Multi-account reporting: If you run multiple accounts or brands, we can report across everything in one place.

Ongoing Monitoring

Google Ads problems can burn through budget quickly. We keep a close eye on things so issues get caught early, not at the end of the month.

  • ✓
    Performance monitoring: We watch for sudden changes in spend, CTR, CPA, or conversion volume and act quickly when something looks off.
  • ✓
    Budget oversight: We track spend against your daily and monthly budgets to make sure nothing runs away from you.
  • ✓
    Ad disapproval checks: If an ad gets disapproved, we diagnose the issue and get it fixed quickly.
