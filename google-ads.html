API-powered campaign management

Our proprietary tools, built on the Google Ads API, automate the operational complexity of campaign management so our strategists can focus on what matters most: performance.

Automated Bid Optimisation

Our platform connects directly to the Google Ads API to monitor campaign performance metrics in real time and make intelligent bid adjustments based on configurable rules and performance thresholds.

  • ✓
    Performance-based bid rules — Automated bid adjustments triggered by CPA, ROAS, conversion rate, and impression share targets.
  • ✓
    Day-parting & device adjustments — Granular bid modifiers applied automatically based on historical performance by time of day, day of week, and device type.
  • ✓
    Budget pacing & allocation — Intelligent budget distribution across campaigns based on marginal return, preventing overspend on underperforming campaigns.

Keyword & Search Term Management

Managing search terms at scale is one of the most time-consuming aspects of Google Ads. Our API-integrated tools automate the heavy lifting while keeping a human strategist in the loop.

  • ✓
    Automated search term analysis — Daily processing of search term reports to identify new keyword opportunities and negative keyword candidates.
  • ✓
    Quality Score monitoring — Tracking keyword Quality Scores over time and flagging opportunities to improve ad relevance, landing page experience, and expected CTR.
  • ✓
    Competitive keyword intelligence — Monitoring auction insights and competitive metrics to inform bidding strategy and identify emerging threats.

Custom Reporting & Dashboards

Transparent, real-time reporting built on live API data — not screenshots from the Google Ads interface. Our clients see exactly how their campaigns are performing at any given moment.

  • ✓
    Real-time performance dashboards — Live campaign data including spend, conversions, CPA, ROAS, and impression share, refreshed via the API.
  • ✓
    Automated weekly & monthly reports — Scheduled report generation with narrative commentary highlighting key trends and recommended actions.
  • ✓
    Cross-account portfolio views — For clients with multiple accounts or brands, unified reporting across the entire portfolio.

Performance Monitoring & Alerts

Problems in Google Ads can burn through budget fast. Our monitoring system catches issues before they become expensive, alerting our team to anomalies and recommending corrective action.

  • ✓
    Anomaly detection — Automated monitoring for sudden changes in spend, CTR, CPA, or conversion volume that fall outside expected ranges.
  • ✓
    Budget alerts — Real-time notifications when campaigns approach or exceed daily or monthly budget thresholds.
  • ✓
    Ad disapproval monitoring — Instant alerts when ads are disapproved, with automated diagnosis and recommended fixes.
