About Alamex Digital

An SEO and Google Ads agency that's been doing this for over a decade.

Why we exist

Most businesses don't need a full-service agency that dabbles in everything. They need people who really know search and can show what it's doing for the bottom line.

We're based in Bath and we've been doing SEO and Google Ads for over ten years. Our team knows the strategy side and the technical side, and we've built our own tools and automation to give clients an edge that manual management can't match.

We work with businesses of all sizes across a range of sectors. What our clients have in common is they want to know what's being done, why it's being done, and whether it's actually working.
