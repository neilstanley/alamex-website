About Alamex Digital

A specialist search marketing agency built on expertise, technology, and transparent partnerships.

Built from experience, driven by results

Alamex Digital was founded on a simple observation: most businesses don't need a full-service agency that spreads itself thin across every marketing channel. They need specialists who understand search deeply and can deliver measurable commercial outcomes.

Based in the heart of Bath, we've spent over a decade refining our approach to SEO and Google Ads management. Our team combines strategic thinking with technical depth — and we've built proprietary tools and automation to give our clients an edge that manual management simply can't replicate.

We work with businesses of all sizes, from ambitious startups to established brands, across a range of sectors. What unites our clients is a desire for transparency, accountability, and results they can measure against their bottom line.

Our values

Depth over breadth

Radical transparency
